Guide

If you have not yet, you need to join a group to share

You can ask your local group to make one, if not, make one yourself

See the navigation bar, there is a 'NEW GROUP' button, click it, then keep the seed as is

It'll show you a seed, you need to share it with whom you want to invite to your group (like friends)

KEEP THE SEED SECRET OTHERWISE ALL THE SECURITY WILL BE COMPROMISED

When new friends come into your group, instead of 'none' use that seed as seed

Setting up users

Antisocial is purely in-memory for amnesic reasons, but if you keep your password and pseudonym private, it'll generate the same keys every time..

Also, it's recommended to save the group seed somewhere (on paper, encrypted text file, etc.) because that'll be required to bootstrap every time too
